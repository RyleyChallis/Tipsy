Cookie Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Definition of Cookies

Cookies are small data files stored on your browser or device. They allow websites to "remember" your actions or preferences over time.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to improve your browsing experience, specifically to facilitate our internal routing system and ensure mobile menu stability.

3. Categorization of Cookies

  • Necessary Cookies: Required for the technical operation of the site, such as the router.js script.
  • Preference Cookies: Used to remember your site settings.
  • Performance Cookies: Used to monitor site traffic and link health.

4. Third-Party Cookies

We do not currently allow third-party tracking or advertising cookies on our website.

5. Managing Your Preferences

You can choose to disable cookies through your individual browser settings. Note that disabling essential cookies may break the navigation logic of this site.
