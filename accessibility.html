Accessibility Statement

1. Commitment to Accessibility

Tipsy Kisses strives to ensure its services are accessible to people with disabilities. We believe every person has the right to live with dignity, equality, and comfort.

2. Technical Specifications

Accessibility on this website relies on the following technologies to work with the particular combination of web browser and any assistive technologies or plugins installed on your computer:

  • HTML
  • WAI-ARIA
  • JavaScript

3. Known Limitations

While we strive for complete accessibility, some sections of our gallery or interactive elements may still be undergoing optimization for screen readers.

4. Standard Compliance

We work to comply with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 level AA standards where possible.

5. Contact for Support

If you are experiencing difficulty with any content on this website, please contact us via our inquiry form so we can assist you.
